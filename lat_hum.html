Humidity


On 1/5/2017, humidity was consistently higher in tropical locations with many locations reaching 100%. Some of these cities include Isla Mujeres, Mexico and Mar del Plata, Argentina. The location that experienced the least humidity was Kerman, Iran at 5%. The average humidity experienced was 70%.
