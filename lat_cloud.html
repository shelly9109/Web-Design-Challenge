Cloudiness


Cloudiness on 1/5/2017 appeared to be fairly even across all latitudes. Christchurch and Kaitangata, New Zealand experienced the worst cloud coverage at 100% along with Vaini and Along, Indonesia and several other cities. The average amount of cloud coverage was 42%.
