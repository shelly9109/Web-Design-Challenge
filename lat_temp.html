Max Temperature


On 1/5/2017, temperatures were higher in the southern hemisphere. As the latitude nears 20 degrees, we see a sharp drop in temperatures throughout the northern hempisphere. This would signify that the southern hemisphere was experiencing summer and the northern hemisphere was experiencing winter due to the tilt of Earth's axis. The data of this data (1/5/17) confirms this. The average temperature was 68F. Although the southern hemisphere was experiencing summer, Mataura, New Zealand experienced the lowest temperature of all cities at 25.69F. Sabha, Libya experienced the highest temperature at 112.45F.
